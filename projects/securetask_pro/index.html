About SecureTask Pro

SecureTask Pro is designed with your privacy in mind, offering a robust platform for managing your daily tasks. While this version demonstrates frontend functionality and local storage persistence, a full production deployment would include strong backend encryption, secure user authentication (JWT), and persistent data storage in a PostgreSQL database.

Our commitment is to combine seamless user experience with top-tier security principles, ensuring your information remains confidential and accessible only to you.
